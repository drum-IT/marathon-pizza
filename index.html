Our Story

THE PASSION FOR PIZZA

Marathon has been serving Hopkinton's finest pizza since 1994, way back during the first Clinton administration. The thing is, we've never stopped improving, just like Windows 95. Our famous pie has kicked it up a flavor notch with a delicious new Italian style crust, a secret blend of delectable cheeses, don't tell anyone, and mom's coveted plum tomato sauce recipe. Come visit us at our new location in scenic downtown Hopkinton for the best pizza, like ever. We love pizza. You love pizza. We have something in common. By the way, thank you for being you.
